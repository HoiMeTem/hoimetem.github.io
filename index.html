About Me

I'm the elven mage of the hero party, the one who slayed the Demon King and brought an era of peace to the world. I've learned over 2000 spells ranging from the Heian era all the way to the modern era, as well as from the many a places I've travelled, hailing from from the coldest and remotest of villages to the most powerful kingdoms and cities. I will be a great addition to your party.

I've killed the most demons of all mages. Aura the Guillotione, Macht the Bringer of End, Solitar the Unknown Great Demon, and the Demon King. None could stand in my way, and now I will uphold this era of peace until the day I die or until demonkind goes extinct, either under outside forces or my own hands.
